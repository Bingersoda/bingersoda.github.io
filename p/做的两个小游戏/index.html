做的两个小游戏

Jun 15, 2025
2 minute read

在上学期的一门游戏课里，我做了好几个小游戏，最后挑了两个出来修改了一下，准备放在这里存个档。

Goose Chaos in Pandemic

In this little game, you play as a rebellious goose causing trouble during the pandemic in China. I got the idea from “Untitled Goose Game” - I really love how the goose creates chaos and makes everyone laugh!

I had this idea when we were still under strict COVID controls in China. My friends and I would often joke around saying, “If I were a goose, what would I do to break free from all these restrictions?” The first thing that came to mind was messing up the daily PCR sites (yes, we had to get tested EVERY single day back then!).

We came up with tons of funny scenarios, though I couldn’t fit all of them into this small game. There’s still a lot from those days that I haven’t included, as I’m still figuring out how to balance the serious history with a lighthearted approach.

If I get the chance in the future, I’d love to keep improving this game and add more of our ideas!
